No Wearing of Earings Inside the Campus for Boys

In line with the Student Manual's Code of Conduct and Discipline, the wearing of earrings by male students is strictly prohibited. As such, please be advised that, effective immediately, male students found in violation of this policy will face appropriate consequences. Furthermore, all school personnel are authorized to confiscate earrings from male students and report the incident to the Office of Student Affairs. Confiscated earrings will be disposed of immediately.
